What buyers ask first

  • Plastic thickness: confirm tray weight and wall thickness by cell type.
  • Stack pressure: verify load-bearing performance in palletized transport.
  • UV durability: define indoor nursery vs outdoor exposure requirement.
  • Nesting efficiency: check pieces per carton and container loading quantity.

Recommended quote details

  • Cell format: required cell count, tray depth, and drainage hole style.
  • Material choice: recycled blend ratio and color preference.
  • Usage cycle: single-season disposable or multi-cycle reusable target.
  • Logistics plan: destination port, shipment split, and buffer stock policy.
