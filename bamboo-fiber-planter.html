What buyers ask first

  • Eco claim proof: clarify bamboo fiber ratio and any available compliance documents.
  • Color consistency: confirm batch tolerance before mixed-color production.
  • Carton protection: define drop-test level to reduce transit damage claims.
  • OEM setup cost: align logo method and mold/plate fee policy upfront.

Recommended quote details

  • Target market: country + sales channel (retail, chain, e-commerce).
  • Required sizes: diameter/height mix and expected quantity per size.
  • Packaging type: bulk carton, retail box, or barcode-ready shelf pack.
  • Trade terms: preferred port, Incoterm, and expected shipment month.
