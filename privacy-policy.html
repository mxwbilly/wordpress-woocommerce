Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-21

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry, we may collect contact details and business information such as your name, company, email, phone/WhatsApp, country, and product requirements.

  • To follow up on sample requests, production planning, and shipping arrangements.
  • To improve website content and user experience.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We may use analytics tools (such as GA4) to measure interactions like product-detail clicks, inquiry submissions, and contact-button clicks. These events are used for performance analysis and conversion optimization.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with trusted service providers (such as hosting, CRM, or email providers) strictly for business operations.

5. Data Retention

Inquiry data is retained only as long as needed for customer service, quotation support, legal compliance, and business records.
